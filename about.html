Corporate Profile

Our History & Pillars

Anima Sana In Corpore Sano — A Sound Mind in a Sound Body.

Our Vision

To create quality lifestyles through intelligent sport technology. We strive to build a sustainable global community where physical empowerment and mental harmony interact seamlessly, making active lifestyles accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality structural goods and biomechanical frameworks that optimize performance vectors. By continuously committing to science, research, and technical sustainability, we ensure our athletes expand boundaries without sacrificing health metrics.

Corporate Origins & Heritage

Founded in 1949 by Kihachiro Onitsuka in Kobe, Japan, ASICS began with a simple yet profound vision: to elevate the health and spirits of youth through sports manufacturing excellence. Over decades, this expanded into a worldwide athletic equipment research phenomenon, spearheaded by the dedicated ASICS Institute of Sport Science.
